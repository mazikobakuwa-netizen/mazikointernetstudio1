Semester Project

For my semester proejct, I want to create a directory like site or automated database for like minded groups to organize, meet, and sign up.
There are many new social groups, such as:

  • run clubs
  • pottery clubs
  • hot yoga groups (different from traditional yoga)
  • reading clubs
  • streaming or content creator groups that even rent homes together for content
and more. This site will help these niche and hobby groups have a generalized place to organize their gatherings and convey group reminders and messages. It will consists of a search bar to find groups, recommendations based on interest survey, a newsletter for accomplishements and news of the groups, an integrated airtable of the list of groups to join, and more.
The inspiration of this plan was due to me being a transfer student and not feeling as if I belonged. It is common for other transfers to not find their footing, which leads to the development of school organizations I am apart of, such as Transfer Student Peer Mentor Program, where I help my mentee explore the DFW area. I hope that others can find ways to belong in a group of their common interest or even step outside their comfort zone when moving to the DFW area.
