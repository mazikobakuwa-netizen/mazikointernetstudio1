Summary of Content

My favorite movies gallery is a compilation of my favorite genres. I am a huge fan of thriller and drama movies, espeically those centerered around criminal pursuits by governement/spy agencies. Because of this, most of the movies I watch either have the same director, producer, story line, or actors. Because of this passion, one would think I am a fan of the Bond series, but I feel that the plots are outdated (ironic based on my repetetive interest). I've found that I am a big fan of movies featuring actors like Liam Neeson, Chris Evans, Tom Hollander, and Jason Stratham,or produced by Harlan Cohen, Tom Clancy, and the likes. The only outlier to this list would be the movie "Me Before You" as it is a childhood favorite.

Favorite Movies

No Time To Die

305 mins

James Bond's wife is suspected of working with Spectre, so they part ways, just to collide paths due to a chemical operation. A chemical is produced to only target those who work with Bond, but is then hijacked by Spectre, just to be re-hijacked by the main chemist who betrayed the team creating the chemical in the first place!

Heads of State

114 mins

The U.S. President and British Prime Minister are framed then presumed dead due to a mole in their camps. They are then targeted to be killed, so that a vote can be passed in a UN gathering. They out aside their differences and save the day while aslo investigating who betrayed them!

Jack Reacher: Never Go Back

113 mins

An armer major is framed for treason, and former army investigator Jack Reacher of the 110th infantry gets wrapped up in the conspiracy of someone who he has only spoken to on the phone, in the midst of Mardi Gras in New Orleans.

Shooter

129 mins

Shooter follows a former military sniper who is framed for an assisination on a government official!

Road House

123 mins

This movie is about a man (Jack Ryan) who moves to a small beach town and becomes a regular at this bar. The bar faces issues with turf wars due to biker gangs. He proceeds to beat up everyone who messes with the bar on his own. He also foils the plans of the biker gangs which leads to a dramatic shoot out scene, where he comes out the hero.

Me Before You

110 mins

This movoe is about a woman who becomes a caretaker for a young man who was paralyzed in a motor accident. The man has a wish to go through with assisted suicide, but falls in love with his caretaker, which derails his plans. Eventually, he goes through with it, but leaves her heartbroken with a hefty inheritance because he was rich!
