Cities to Explore

Bali has a variety of cities, each with their own charm and attractions. Ubud is known for it's art and culture, and is home to the Tegenungan Waterfall and Ubud Monkey Forest. Seminyak offers upscale shopping, art, dining, and nightlife in areas like Seminyak Square and Nyaman Gallery. Kuta is famous for its vibrant beach scene and surfing spots. It is home to resorts such as Waterbom Bali and shopping experiences at Beachwalk Shopping Center. Jimbaran is renowned for its seafood restaurants along the beach, while Nusa Dua boasts luxury resorts and pristine beaches.Each soutch coast city offers snorkeling, yatching, ATV rides, and surfing being popular activities. Here are the must-visit cities in Bali:

  • Ubud
  • Seminyak
  • Kuta
  • Jimbaran
  • Nusa Dua

Attractions

  • Tegenungan Waterfall
  • Uluwatu Temple
  • Tanah Lot
  • Ubud Monkey Forest
  • Mount Batur
